Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that layers sheets of pasta with rich, savory fillings to create a hearty and comforting meal. Traditionally, it features a robust meat sauce made from ground beef or pork simmered with tomatoes, garlic, and onions, paired with a creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese mixture.


Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano or Italian seasoning
