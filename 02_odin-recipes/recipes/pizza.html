Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy, featuring a round, flat dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings like meats, vegetables, and herbs. Baked until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly, pizza is known for its delicious flavor and endless customization options. Whether enjoyed with classic toppings like pepperoni and mozzarella or creative ones like pineapple or arugula, pizza is a universal favorite that's perfect for any meal or gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
