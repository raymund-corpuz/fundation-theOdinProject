Pasta

Description

Pasta is a versatile and widely loved dish that originated in Italy and is made from a simple dough of wheat flour and water or eggs. It comes in many shapes and sizes, such as spaghetti, penne, or lasagna, each pairing well with different types of sauces. Whether served with a rich tomato sauce, creamy Alfredo, or tossed with olive oil and vegetables, pasta is a comforting, satisfying meal enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) dried pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne, fusilli)
  • Water (for boiling)
  • 1 tablespoon salt (for boiling water)
  • 2 cups pasta sauce (tomato, Alfredo, pesto, etc.)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for topping)
  • Olive oil or butter (optional)
  • Optional add-ins: garlic, onion, ground meat, vegetables, herbs
